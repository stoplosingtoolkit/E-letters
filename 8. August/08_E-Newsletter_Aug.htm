Food For Success


No matter what your job is, you should always have enough needed energy to perform it. Eat well - you won’t get fat, but you have to eat as much as you need in order to not feel hungry while at work. That will clearly affect your ability to do the job.

Therefore, a day of your work life should be divided into stages that can ensure you get the proper nutrients for peak performance. It doesn’t matter if you work from 9 AM to 5 PM or if you work all night.

Long Run Persons

If you are a "long run person," you are on your feet the entire day so the amount of physical effort you are making is significant. Your body needs energy from morning until evening. You must keep the sugar levels stable with the help of complex hydrocarbonates. You will be like a little ant if you start out your day with cereal and milk, you eat for lunch soup and vegetables with cheese and in the evening you can save yourself some lasagna and salad.

Night Stars

If you are a "night star" your work hours keep going until the wee morning hours. You need food for feeling okay and happy, combating depression that can often occur during nights. Vitamin B1 is the one that will keep you smiling all night. Your day should start out with a breakfast of bread and honey and the lunch should consist of pasta and vegetables. You can end your day with a dinner of barbecue chicken and salad.

Office Angel

If you are an "office angel" then you are performing intense mental activities and a low physical effort. Your body needs "intelligent" food, that would give energy to the brain and nerves, that means nutrients rich in vitamins B and C. For breakfasts you can eat bananas and yogurt, at lunch scrambled eggs and toasted bread and for dinner potatoes, fish and vegetables.
